What Is Opheilo?

What Is Opheilo Embassy?

Opheilo Embassy is a family of ambassadors who believe that every area of life has an “ought” according to the heart of God.

The Christian’s marriage ought to be lived a certain way according to the heart of God. The Christian’s business ought to be run a certain way according to the heart of God. The Church ought to live and love a certain way according to the heart of God.

Kingdom Definition for Every Sphere

We believe Jesus is not only Savior, but King, and His Kingdom brings definition to how life is meant to be lived.

We also believe the Church, as seen in the book of Acts, was never meant to be limited to a single expression, but to function as a living, active body moving in both gathered and sent forms.

We are not opposed to the modern church expression. We believe it plays a vital role. But we also believe there must be both gathered spaces and sent expressions working together.

Opheilo exists as a complementary expression, a mobilized extension that helps carry the life of the Church into everyday environments across the city.

We are building a people who live as ambassadors of Jesus Christ, representing His nature, His authority, and His Kingdom in every sphere of life.

Our Model

Formation in Homes. Expression in the City.

House Annexes (Formation)

A house annex is a smaller extension of a larger body, designed for intentional development and focused growth.

Our house annexes are small, intimate communities where disciples are formed in the context of real relationship and shared life.

  • Deeply discipled
  • Grounded in Scripture
  • Shaped in character
  • Rooted in prayer and shared life

This is where transformation happens.

Business Embassies (Expression)

Our business embassies are public-facing environments embedded throughout the city.

  • Engage people where they already are
  • Create natural opportunities for connection
  • Demonstrate kingdom culture in real environments
  • Serve as points of outreach and influence

This is where disciples are sent.

We disciple in homes and deploy in the city.
Our Mission

Our Mission

We exist to make disciples who are completely transformed, shaped, and mobilized by the Gospel of Jesus Christ making His love and power made known to all men.

We believe that Jesus is King and He has a desire for His Church to live in a specific manner, with a specific revelation, moving with a specific dominion.

We are committed to fulfilling His desires by His Spirit, that all that ought to be will be established in the city, according to His heart.

Our Vision

Our Vision

We envision a city filled with house embassies where disciples are developed, trained, and equipped to walk in deep intimacy with Jesus and carry His light into every place of darkness.

Through these communities, ambassadors of Christ will be raised to influence culture, demonstrate the power of God, and prepare the city for His coming.

Why We Exist

Why We Exist

We exist because we are men and women after the heart of God.

Our Embassies in the City

Businesses That Open Doors for Transformation

Each embassy is a fully functioning business designed to engage real needs, create meaningful environments, and open the door for transformation in everyday life.

Hawkins House

A Christ-centered family development initiative focused on restoring and strengthening the foundation of the home.

Hawkins House equips parents to intentionally build a culture of discipleship within their families through clear, comprehensive pathways. It helps transform the home from a place of routine into a place of formation, where faith is lived, modeled, and multiplied across generations.

The Rally Point

A dynamic business ecosystem designed for purpose-driven entrepreneurs and builders.

The Rally Point brings together business incubation, innovation, and inspiration within a unique environment shaped around the sport of pickleball. It creates space for visionaries to grow, collaborate, and build ventures that are both economically strong and missionally aligned.

The Connection Café & Bakery

A café and bakery experience designed to restore the depth of human connection.

By thoughtfully leveraging technology, The Connection Café creates guided and intentional interactions that move beyond surface-level conversation. It serves as a platform where meaningful relationships can form naturally in a space people already love to gather.

Home-Work Youth Empowerment

An inner-city youth initiative focused on developing the whole person while preparing the next generation for real opportunity.

Home-Work prioritizes holistic wellbeing while equipping young people with leadership skills, purpose, and practical readiness for the workforce. It exists to empower youth with the vision, tools, and confidence needed to build a viable future and step into economic mobility.

Each embassy meets people where they are, while opening a pathway to who they are becoming.
